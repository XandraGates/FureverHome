About Furever Home

Here at Furever Home, we believe that a home is for life - not a temporary resting stop. We seek to rehome our animals within loving, stable environments. While we specialise in rehoming cats and dogs, we often have a selection of rodents and birds in need of a family. To find out more about our current host of animals, please get in touch. You can contact us here.


FAQs

What makes a good 'Furever Home'?

In addition to a stable and loving environment, we seek to rehome our animals with a family that can best fulfil their individual needs. For example, if animals of a nervous nature are often better suited in households where everyone is over the age of 16. We always carry out home inspections as an indication of suitability.

Are you hiring / looking for volunteers?

We are currently not seeking any more staff. Please check in with your other local shelters though!

Where can I find the 'Furever Home'?

Unfortunately, Furever Home does not exist. It was created for a coding assignment. There are, however, many wonderful charities out there that seek to rehome animals. This one was inspired by Nash's Furever Haus, which seeks to connect elderly dogs with families, as well as providing them with the love and medical care many so desperately need during their stay.
